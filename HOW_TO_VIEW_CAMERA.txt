HOW TO VIEW CAMERA FEED ON FTC DRIVER STATION
==============================================

You mentioned the camera status and FPS are showing in telemetry, which means the camera is working! 
Now you need to view the actual video feed on the Driver Station screen.

METHOD 1: USING THE CAMERA STREAM BUTTON (Most Common)
-------------------------------------------------------

1. START YOUR OPMODE:
   - Select "MaximumDriveV4Beta" from TeleOp
   - Press INIT
   - Press START

2. LOOK FOR THE CAMERA ICON:
   - On the Driver Station screen, look for a CAMERA ICON (usually looks like 📷)
   - This icon typically appears:
     * In the top-right corner of the screen
     * Near the telemetry display
     * In a toolbar at the top or bottom

3. TAP THE CAMERA ICON:
   - Tap the camera icon to open the camera view
   - The video feed should appear either:
     * In a small preview window (which you can expand)
     * As a full-screen overlay
     * In a separate section of the screen

4. IF YOU DON'T SEE THE CAMERA ICON:
   - Swipe down from the top of the screen (like opening notifications)
   - Look in the settings/menu (three dots or hamburger menu)
   - Try tapping on the telemetry area - some versions show camera there


METHOD 2: USING CAMERA STREAM MENU
-----------------------------------

1. While OpMode is running, tap the THREE DOTS (⋮) menu
2. Look for "Camera Stream" or "View Camera"
3. Select it to open the camera feed


METHOD 3: DS APP VERSIONS
--------------------------

Different Driver Station app versions show camera differently:

**DS Version 8.0+:**
- Camera preview appears automatically in a dedicated section
- Tap it to expand to full screen

**DS Version 7.x:**
- Look for camera icon in top-right corner
- May need to enable "Show Camera Stream" in settings

**DS Version 6.x and older:**
- May not support live camera preview
- SOLUTION: Update to latest DS version from Google Play Store


VIEWING OPTIONS ONCE CAMERA IS OPEN
------------------------------------

FULL SCREEN MODE:
- Tap the video feed once it's visible
- Should expand to fill the screen
- Telemetry may minimize or move to the side

SPLIT SCREEN MODE:
- Some DS versions let you see both telemetry AND camera
- Look for a "split" or "layout" option in the camera view
- Or pinch/zoom to resize the camera window

PICTURE-IN-PICTURE:
- Camera appears as small window over telemetry
- You can usually drag it around the screen
- Tap to expand/collapse


TROUBLESHOOTING: "I STILL CAN'T SEE VIDEO"
-------------------------------------------

ISSUE: Camera icon appears but video is black
FIX:
- The camera might be initializing
- Wait 5-10 seconds after starting OpMode
- Check that your telemetry shows "Camera State: STREAMING" (not OPENING)

ISSUE: No camera icon anywhere
FIX:
- Update Driver Station app to latest version
- Check DS app settings for "Enable Camera Stream" or similar
- Your DS version might be too old for VisionPortal

ISSUE: Camera works in Robot Controller but not on DS
FIX:
- Check WiFi connection quality
- Try reducing camera resolution in code (640x480 or 320x240)
- Bandwidth might be limited - camera uses a lot of data

ISSUE: Laggy or frozen video
FIX:
- Move Driver Station closer to Robot Controller
- Lower camera resolution in code
- Close other apps on DS device


ALTERNATIVE: VIEW ON ROBOT CONTROLLER WEB INTERFACE
----------------------------------------------------

If Driver Station won't show the feed, you can view it on a computer:

1. Connect computer to Robot Controller WiFi
2. Open browser and go to: http://192.168.43.1:8080
3. You should see the camera stream in the web interface
4. This is useful for debugging but not practical during a match


CHECKING IF CAMERA IS ACTUALLY STREAMING
-----------------------------------------

Based on your telemetry:
✓ Camera FPS: (showing a number) = Camera is capturing frames
✓ Camera State: STREAMING = Camera is working

If these are showing correctly, the camera IS working. The issue is just viewing 
it on the Driver Station screen.


DRIVER STATION APP UPDATE
--------------------------

If you're having trouble, make sure your DS app is up to date:

1. Open Google Play Store (or equivalent)
2. Search for "FTC Driver Station"
3. If "Update" button shows, tap it
4. Restart DS app after updating

Recommended minimum version: 8.0 or newer for full VisionPortal support


QUICK CHECKLIST
---------------

□ OpMode is running (not just initialized)
□ Telemetry shows camera FPS and "STREAMING"
□ Looked for camera icon in all corners of screen
□ Tried tapping on telemetry area
□ Checked three-dots menu for camera option
□ DS app is version 8.0 or newer
□ WiFi connection is strong
□ Waited at least 10 seconds after starting OpMode


STILL STUCK?
------------

Try this test:
1. In Robot Configuration, note your camera name: "Webcam123"
2. Run MaximumDriveV4Beta
3. Check telemetry - does it show FPS? (If yes, camera works)
4. Take a photo of your DS screen and note which DS version you're using
5. This will help diagnose if it's a DS app issue vs camera issue

Remember: If telemetry shows FPS and STREAMING, your camera is 100% working!
The issue is just making the DS app display the video feed to you.
